Our Services

Complete Tests At Morya Lab for Good Health

Diabetes Testing

Manage your health with our comprehensive diabetes testing. We measure blood glucose levels to help you monitor your risk and manage diabetes effectively.


Kidney Tests

Assess your kidney health with our kidney function tests. These tests evaluate key markers to ensure your kidneys are performing optimally, helping to prevent potential complications.

Thyroid Profile

Stay on top of your hormonal health with our thyroid profile. We analyze thyroid hormones to diagnose conditions like hypothyroidism or hyperthyroidism, ensuring balanced well-being.

Lipid Profile

Understand your cardiovascular health with our lipid profile. This test measures cholesterol and triglyceride levels, helping you assess your risk for heart disease.


Liver Function Tests

Protect your liver with our liver function tests. We evaluate enzymes and other markers to assess liver health, crucial for detoxification and overall wellness.

Urine Test

Our urine tests provide valuable insights into your health. By analyzing various components, we can detect infections, kidney issues, and metabolic conditions.

Stool Test

Monitor your digestive health with our stool tests. These tests help identify gastrointestinal issues, infections, or malabsorption, guiding you to better gut health.

Heart Profile Test

Prioritize your heart health with our heart profile test. This comprehensive assessment evaluates cholesterol, inflammation markers, and more to assess your cardiovascular risk.
